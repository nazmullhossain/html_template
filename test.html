# Collage Maker – Photo Editor (Flutter Android & iOS)

Collage Maker – Photo Editor is a complete Flutter app that allows users to create beautiful photo collages and edit images with powerful tools. The app comes with modern layouts, stylish fonts, stunning filters, and creative editing options. Perfect for social media creators and users who want professional-looking photos with ease.

Key Features

  • Layouts & Templates: Create photo collages with multiple images and pre-designed templates.
  • Image Editing: Crop, resize, rotate, flip, and blur images with ease.
  • Backgrounds: Choose from beautiful backgrounds and solid colors.
  • Filters & Effects: Apply professional filters, frames, and adjustments (brightness, contrast, saturation, hue).
  • Text Tools: Add stylish text with 40+ fonts, neon styles, curve text, opacity control, shadows, and formatting (bold, italic, underline, line-through).
  • Stickers & Emojis: Add stickers, tattoos, and emojis to your photos.
  • Save & Share: Save edited photos to your device or share instantly on social media.

Advanced Editing Options

  • Adjust text and background colors
  • Apply shadows for depth effects
  • Customize opacity and style settings
  • Flexible collage builder with unlimited image support

Why Choose This App?

This Flutter-based collage maker and photo editor app is fully customizable, works on both Android and iOS, and is easy to set up. Whether for personal use or as a commercial app, it offers all the essential features to create high-quality, creative photo collages.
